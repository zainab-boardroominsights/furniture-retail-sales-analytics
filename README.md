# Furniture Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Overview

This project is an executive-level retail analytics dashboard developed in Looker Studio using Excel as the primary data source.

The dashboard was designed to provide leadership teams with visibility into sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, store performance, and operational trends across a multi-store furniture retail business.

The objective of this project was to transform fragmented retail sales data into a centralized business intelligence solution that supports faster and more informed decision-making.

---

## Business Problem

Retail businesses often struggle with:
- fragmented sales reporting
- limited visibility into store performance
- inconsistent profitability tracking
- lack of customer retention insights
- difficulty monitoring sales team performance

This dashboard was created to address these challenges by consolidating operational and financial KPIs into an interactive executive reporting solution.

---

## Key Insights

- Dubai Mall emerged as the top-performing store by overall revenue contribution.
- Repeat customer contribution remained strong despite slower sales growth trends.
- Higher discount percentages showed limited impact on long-term revenue growth.
- Premium product categories demonstrated stronger profitability and customer retention trends.
- Gross profit margins remained relatively stable across reporting periods.
